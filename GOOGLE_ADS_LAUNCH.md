# Veritor Group — Google Ads Launch Plan

Consolidated campaign blueprint based on keyword research, competitive
landscape, and audience analysis. Built for paste-into-Google-Ads
execution. Source agents documented their primary references inline;
cross-checked against FMCSA SAFER, BLS OES 53-3032, ATRI 2023 cost
report, BTS Freight Facts 2024, Google autocomplete (live), and the
TruckersReport corpus.

---

## TL;DR

- **Search volume is small.** The English seller-intent universe is a
  few thousand monthly US searches across all relevant terms combined.
  Search alone won't scale to 400-acquisition volume. Use Search to
  capture *high-intent* demand; layer YouTube + RLSA + Performance Max
  + Meta lookalikes to scale.
- **Veritor's moat is the combination**: operator-led + US-domestic +
  400+ closed + 48-hr written offer + 2-week close + EN/ES/RU. No
  single competitor matches more than two of those six.
- **Phase 1 = Search-only, $4,500–$6,000/month, 5 ad groups (3 EN +
  1 ES + 1 RU), Manual CPC.** Add YouTube + RLSA after 30 days, PMax
  after 60 days conditional on data.
- **Realistic month-1 outcome**: 15–25 qualified leads, CPL $200–$500,
  ≥1 LOI signed.

**One Search campaign with 5 ad groups, paused on launch:**

|---|---|---|---|
| A. Sell — Amazon Relay | Exact only | `/` | National + GA/OH/NC priority |
| B. Sell — Fresh MC (under 180d) | Exact + Phrase | `/` | National |
| C. Sell — General Logistics LLC | Exact + Phrase | `/` | National |
| D. Sell — Spanish | Exact + Phrase | `/es` | TX/CA/FL/AZ |
| E. Sell — Russian | Exact + Phrase | `/ru` | Sacramento/NJ/Chicago/Miami |

**Why 5 not 10:** lower volume = data scarcity. Tighter ad groups would
strangle Smart Bidding when we hand it the keys at 30+ conv. Five gives
each group enough oxygen to build conversion history.

**Skip Performance Max in Phase 1.** PMax cannibalizes brand search and
needs conversion volume to learn. Revisit at day 60 if Search hits ≥30
conv/30 days.

## 7. Bid strategy

| Stage | Trigger | Strategy | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| Day 0–14 | 0 conversions | **Manual CPC**, $3–$6 max bid, Enhanced CPC OFF | Smart Bidding has no signal yet — would burn budget chasing broad signals. |
| Day 15–30 | 5–14 conversions | Manual CPC continues | Watch search-terms report daily. Add negatives. |
| Day 31+ | ≥15 conv in 30 rolling days | **Maximize Conversions** (no tCPA cap) | Let it learn for 2 weeks. |
| Day 45+ | ≥30 conv in 30 rolling days | **Target CPA** at 80% of observed CPA | Now Smart Bidding has enough data. |

A closed acquisition is worth $5K–$50K+ on the buy-side. If close rate
is even 5%, defending a $300–$600 CPL is mathematically sound.

## 12. Honest caveats

- **CPC/CPL ranges are estimates**, not measured data — bracket
  conservatively and recalibrate after 14 days of search-terms data.
- **Spanish/Russian search volume is small** — capture demand when it
  appears, but don't expect Search to drive volume in those languages.
  Real ES/RU scale comes from YouTube + Meta lookalikes.
- **Search alone won't hit 400-acquisition pace.** It captures
  high-intent demand cleanly; for scale, layer Meta + YouTube +
  outbound (direct mail) on top.
- **Volume may force ad-group consolidation.** If Ad Group A (Relay)
  averages <2 clicks/day after 14 days, fold it into Ad Group C with a
  Relay-specific RSA variant.
