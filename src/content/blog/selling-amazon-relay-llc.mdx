An LLC that already holds an active Amazon Relay contract is, in plain terms, the most valuable
configuration we buy. The contract is the asset. Everything else — the MC number, the trucks,
the dispatcher relationships — is replaceable. The contract isn't.

## Why Relay LLCs close faster

Three reasons.

First, **the diligence is shorter**. We don't need to age your MC authority or guess whether
you'll be approved. The Amazon dashboard tells us what we need to know in five minutes.

Second, **insurance flexibility**. We buy Relay LLCs whether the underlying insurance policy is
active or has lapsed. As long as the LLC itself is in good standing, we can re-bind coverage as
part of closing.

Third, **the price is more predictable**. Relay LLCs trade in a tighter band, which means
fewer rounds of back-and-forth on valuation.

## What we still verify

- That the Amazon contract is current and not in a probation/cure period
- That the MC number is in good standing on FMCSA
- That violation history is clean — or that any violations are already resolved
- That the LLC's federal/state filings are up to date

## What transfers at closing

Same as any other deal: the LLC itself, the company phone number, the company email, the
company bank account, and the MC/DOT records. The Amazon Relay contract transfers along with
the LLC, since it lives on the LLC.

## Loans

Active loans on the trucks or the company are not a deal-breaker. Disclose them up front and
we'll work the payoff into the structure. We've closed deals with five-figure outstanding
balances and we've closed deals with zero — both work.

## Timeline

For Relay LLCs, our average close is 7–10 days from the time we receive your details. The
bottleneck is usually the bank, not us.
